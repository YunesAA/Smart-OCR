About OCR and AI Cleanup

What We Do

Our application combines Optical Character Recognition (OCR) with artificial intelligence to improve text extraction from images. Whether you have scanned documents, photos of text, or any image containing written content, our tool helps you extract and clean up the text automatically.

How It Works

  1. Upload your image containing text
  2. Our OCR system extracts the raw text
  3. AI processing cleans up any errors or inconsistencies
  4. Get your improved, cleaned text result

Technologies

  • AI Enhancement: Advanced Language Models
  • Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript
  • Backend: Node.js
